I am a PhD researcher exploring Responsible AI governance in start-ups at Loughborough University London. My research interests include Responsible AI, organisational implementation, innovation, and public policy.

Before beginning my PhD, I completed an MBA at the University of Edinburgh and spent several years as an entrepreneur in the UK e-commerce sector. I also worked for 5 years in supply chain and project management in the photovoltaic industry and hold professional certifications, including Certified Supply Chain Professional (CSCP) and Project Management Professional (PMP).
