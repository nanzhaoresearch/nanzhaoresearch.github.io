# Nan Zhao

I am a PhD researcher exploring Responsible AI governance in start-ups, with a strong interest in public policy, responsible innovation, and evidence-informed decision-making.

My research interests include Responsible AI, organisational implementation, innovation, entrepreneurship, and public policy. I am particularly interested in how organisations translate high-level AI governance principles into everyday practices.

This website brings together my developing research, policy learning, and reflections from my PhD journey. As my work develops, I hope to use this space to document what I am learning, share accessible explanations of AI governance concepts, and reflect on how research can inform policy and practice.

## Current Interests

- Responsible AI governance in start-ups
- AI regulation and public policy
- Evidence-informed decision-making
- Responsible innovation
- Research communication for non-specialist audiences
