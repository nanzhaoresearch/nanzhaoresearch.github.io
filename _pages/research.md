## Current research

My PhD research explores how AI start-ups understand and implement
Responsible AI governance while responding to commercial pressures and
evolving regulatory expectations.

I am particularly interested in how principles such as fairness,
transparency and accountability are translated into organisational
practices.

As an early-stage researcher, I am currently refining the project's
conceptual framework and research design.

Detailed methods and findings will be shared through appropriate academic
outputs as the project develops.
