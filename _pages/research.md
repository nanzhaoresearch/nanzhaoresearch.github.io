This page gathers my current and developing research interests. As I am still in the early stage of my PhD, these projects are evolving.

## Current PhD Research

My PhD explores Responsible AI governance in start-ups. I am interested in how start-ups understand, interpret, and implement Responsible AI principles in practice.

Rather than looking only at regulation or technical standards, my research focuses on the organisational side of AI governance: how people, processes, resources, incentives, and business pressures shape responsible AI implementation.

## Policy Learning Notebook

I am also developing a policy learning notebook alongside my PhD. This is a space for exploring how research evidence can inform public policy, especially in areas related to AI governance, innovation, and emerging technologies.

Possible topics include:

- What is Responsible AI governance?
- How do regulatory sandboxes support innovation?
- What does meaningful human oversight mean?
- How can research evidence support better AI policy?
- Why is implementation important in technology governance?
